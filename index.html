Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 17, 2026

Vice ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that uses your device's compass and location to help you find nearby places for your favourite indulgences. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Password — stored as a secure hash by our authentication provider (Supabase). We never see your plaintext password.
  • Display name and username — shown on your profile within the app.

Profile information

  • Date of birth — collected once during onboarding to verify you meet our minimum age requirement. Used only to gate age-restricted content (alcohol, tobacco) within the app.
  • Profile photo — uploaded at your choice and stored in secure cloud storage. You can remove it at any time from your profile.

Location data

  • Precise GPS coordinates — collected while the app is in use to power the compass and to find places near you. Location is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of a search request.
  • Your coordinates are transmitted to Google (via the Google Places API) solely to return nearby place results. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Usage data

  • Vice search history — the categories and custom queries you search (e.g. "Coffee", "Cocktail bar"). Stored so we can show your recent searches and generate aggregate stats for your profile.
  • Vice logs — when you manually log a vice (category, quantity, timestamp). Stored to power your Vice History chart.
  • Favourites — places and vice categories you save. Stored to power your saved-items list.
  • Distance unit preference (miles / kilometres) — stored to personalise your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To operate the compass and return nearby place results relevant to your search.
  • To display your profile, stats, and history within the app.
  • To enforce our minimum age requirement for age-restricted content.
  • To improve the app through aggregate, anonymised usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third-party processors, and only to the extent necessary to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is hosted on servers located in the United States. Supabase's privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Places API — your location and search query are sent to Google to retrieve nearby place results. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not share your information with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Vice logs, search history, and favourites are retained until you delete your account or remove individual entries within the app.
  • Profile photos are deleted from storage when you remove them in the app or delete your account.

5. Children's Privacy

Vice is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. The app contains content related to alcohol and tobacco; users are required to confirm they meet the minimum legal age in their jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information via the Settings screen in the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via your device's system settings.

Residents of the European Economic Area (EEA) and California (CCPA) may have additional rights. To exercise any right, email us at hudsondellis0@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and row-level security policies on our database to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For material changes, we will notify you within the app.
